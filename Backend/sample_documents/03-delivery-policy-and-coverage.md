# Delivery Policy and Service Coverage

Company: Atlas Container Supply
Last updated: 2026-03-20

## Where We Deliver
We deliver throughout the continental United States with strongest lane coverage in:
- Texas
- Oklahoma
- Louisiana
- Arkansas
- New Mexico

Yes, we deliver to Texas daily from Dallas, Houston, San Antonio, and El Paso partner depots.

## Delivery Fees (Typical)
- 0-25 miles from depot: $180-$320
- 26-75 miles: $320-$590
- 76-150 miles: custom lane quote
- Rural/off-grid/limited access sites may include access surcharge

## Delivery Requirements
- Clear truck access: 12 ft width, 14 ft height, 80-120 ft straight approach preferred
- Level placement area
- Ground tolerance for loaded tilt-bed truck and container weight
- On-site contact available by phone at time of drop

## Placement and Offloading
- Standard method: tilt-bed slide-off
- Optional: crane placement via approved third-party carrier
- Driver can reposition within reasonable safety limits during delivery

## Delivery Timelines
- In-stock containers: usually 2-5 business days
- Modified containers: usually 2-4 weeks depending on scope
- Peak season and weather can affect appointment windows

## Redelivery and Failed Delivery
If site conditions prevent safe drop-off, a redelivery fee may apply.
Typical redelivery fee: $150-$300 plus any additional mileage.

## Best-Practice Sales Prompt
When a prospect asks, "Do you deliver to Texas?", confirm yes, then request zip code to provide exact delivered pricing and lead time.
