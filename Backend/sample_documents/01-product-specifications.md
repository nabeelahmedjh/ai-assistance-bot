# Product Specifications - Standard Containers (2026)

Company: Atlas Container Supply
Last updated: 2026-03-15

## 20ft Standard Dry Container
- Exterior dimensions: 20' L x 8' W x 8'6" H
- Interior dimensions: 19'4" L x 7'8" W x 7'10" H
- Door opening: 7'8" W x 7'6" H
- Tare weight: ~4,900 lb
- Max payload: ~62,000 lb
- Cubic capacity: ~1,170 cu ft
- Typical use: compact storage, dense cargo, job-site tool lockups

## 40ft Standard Dry Container
- Exterior dimensions: 40' L x 8' W x 8'6" H
- Interior dimensions: 39'5" L x 7'8" W x 7'10" H
- Door opening: 7'8" W x 7'6" H
- Tare weight: ~8,250 lb
- Max payload: ~59,000 lb
- Cubic capacity: ~2,390 cu ft
- Typical use: inventory overflow, farm equipment, long-term storage

## 40ft High Cube Container
- Exterior dimensions: 40' L x 8' W x 9'6" H
- Interior dimensions: 39'5" L x 7'8" W x 8'10" H
- Cubic capacity: ~2,690 cu ft
- Typical use: furniture, stacked cartons, modified office builds

## Condition Grades We Sell
- One-trip: Near-new condition, one international cargo journey, best for customer-facing locations.
- Cargo-worthy (CW): Wind/water tight and structurally sound with visible cosmetic wear.
- As-is: Lower cost option for non-critical use; may include dents, patches, and surface rust.

## Standard Features
- Marine-grade Corten steel walls
- 1-1/8 in marine plywood floor over steel cross members
- Forklift pockets on 20ft models
- Lockbox-ready cargo doors (lockbox optional)
- Weather-sealed double swing doors

## Recommended Product Pairing
- Need less than 1,300 cu ft and easier placement? Recommend 20ft.
- Need maximum storage value per dollar? Recommend 40ft standard.
- Need extra interior headroom for shelving or conversion? Recommend 40ft high cube.
