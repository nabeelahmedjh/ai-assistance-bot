# FAQ - Sales, Delivery, and Ownership

Company: Atlas Container Supply
Last updated: 2026-03-25

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much is a 40ft container?
Typical 40ft pricing:
- One-trip: about $3,950
- Cargo-worthy: about $3,250
- As-is: about $2,350
Final delivered price depends on zip code and access conditions.

### Do you deliver to Texas?
Yes. We deliver statewide in Texas, including major metro and rural routes. Share your zip code for delivered pricing and lead time.

### What is the difference between new and one-trip?
True brand-new containers are uncommon and usually cost more. One-trip units are near-new and are the most common premium option.

### Are containers waterproof?
One-trip and CW units are sold as wind and water tight unless otherwise noted.

### Can I choose container color?
Yes, standard inventory colors vary by depot. Repaint options are available.

### Do I need a permit?
Permit rules depend on city/county zoning. Buyer is responsible for local compliance.

### Can I finance a container?
Financing is available through partner lenders for qualified business buyers.

### How fast can you deliver?
In-stock units are typically delivered in 2-5 business days, depending on route capacity.

### Can I return a container?
All sales are generally final after delivery. If a material condition issue is reported within 24 hours, support will review for resolution.

## Sales Intent Signal
High-intent phrases include:
- "I need this this week"
- "Can you send an invoice today?"
- "I want to place an order"
When detected, prioritize quote-to-order steps and collect contact details.
