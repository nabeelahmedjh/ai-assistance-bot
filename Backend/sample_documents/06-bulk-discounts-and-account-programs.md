# Bulk Discounts and Account Programs

Company: Atlas Container Supply
Last updated: 2026-03-30

## Volume Discount Tiers (Unit Price Discount)
- 2-4 units: 3% off base unit price
- 5-9 units: 6% off base unit price
- 10-24 units: 9% off base unit price
- 25+ units: custom enterprise pricing and reserved inventory options

## Multi-Site Delivery Support
For projects shipping to multiple addresses, we provide:
- Consolidated quoting by site
- Staggered delivery schedule
- Single account manager
- Weekly order status reports

## Who Typically Uses Bulk Programs
- Construction firms
- Energy and utility contractors
- School districts
- Municipal and emergency response programs
- Self-storage operators

## Payment Terms (Approved Accounts)
- Standard: payment due before dispatch
- Approved commercial accounts: Net 15 or Net 30 on qualifying orders
- Government/education purchase orders accepted with account review

## Reservation and Hold Policy
- Standard hold: 7 days with quote
- Project hold (10+ units): up to 21 days with deposit
