# Quote, Order, and Payment Process

Company: Atlas Container Supply
Last updated: 2026-03-27

## Step 1 - Fast Quote
To issue an accurate quote, collect:
- Container size (20ft, 40ft, or 40ft high cube)
- Condition (one-trip, CW, as-is)
- Delivery zip code
- Intended use (storage, office, workshop, etc.)
- Preferred delivery window

## Step 2 - Quote Structure
A complete quote includes:
- Unit price
- Modifications (if any)
- Delivery line item
- Estimated delivery date range
- Quote expiration date

## Step 3 - Reservation and Invoice
- Standard retail orders: full payment before dispatch
- Large project orders: deposit to reserve inventory
- Accepted payment methods: ACH, wire, business check, major card (processing fees may apply)

## Step 4 - Delivery Confirmation
- Dispatch confirms date/time window
- Customer confirms site readiness and contact person
- Driver calls ahead before arrival

## Post-Delivery Support
- Report any condition concerns within 24 hours with photos
- Warranty support available for eligible modification work

## Conversion Playbook for Sales Assistant
1. Answer question with sourced facts.
2. Offer two fit options (good/better) with transparent tradeoffs.
3. Ask for zip code to provide delivered pricing.
4. Ask for intended use to recommend optional upgrades.
5. Offer next step: same-day quote or invoice-ready order.
